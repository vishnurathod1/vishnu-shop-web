Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with VSV ShopEasy

Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is simple and secure:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Click on the cart icon and proceed to checkout
  3. Enter your shipping address and contact details
  4. Choose your preferred payment method
  5. Review your order and confirm payment

You'll receive an order confirmation email with tracking details once your order is placed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Net Banking
  • PayPal
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) - Available on select products

All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption for your safety.

Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

Absolutely! Your security is our top priority. We use:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
  • Two-factor authentication for added security
  • Regular security audits and updates

We never store your complete card details on our servers.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it:

  • Go to "My Orders" in your account
  • Click on "Modify Order" or "Cancel Order"
  • Follow the prompts to make changes

Note: Once an order is shipped, modifications are not possible. Contact customer service for assistance.

Shipping & Delivery

What are your shipping options and delivery times?

We offer multiple shipping options:

  • Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days - Free on orders over ₹999
  • Express Delivery: 2-3 business days - ₹99 extra
  • Same Day Delivery: Available in select cities - ₹199 extra

Delivery times may vary based on your location and product availability.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our shipping services to international locations soon.

All orders are shipped from our warehouses in India.

How can I track my order?

Tracking your order is easy:

  1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Click on the order you want to track
  3. View real-time tracking updates

You'll also receive tracking updates via SMS and email as your order progresses.

What if I'm not available to receive my delivery?

If you're not available:

  • Our delivery partner will attempt delivery 3 times
  • You can reschedule delivery through the tracking link
  • Leave delivery instructions in your account settings
  • Opt for pickup from a nearby pickup point

After 3 failed attempts, the package will be returned to our warehouse.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free return policy:

  • 30-day return window from the date of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Free returns on defective or wrong items
  • Return shipping fee may apply for change-of-mind returns

Some items like perishables, personal care products, and custom orders are non-returnable.

How do I return an item?

To initiate a return:

  1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the item you want to return
  3. Click "Return Item" and select the reason
  4. Choose pickup or drop-off option
  5. Pack the item and attach the return label

We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item.
