Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about our shipping options, delivery times, and policies

Express Shipping

Delivery Time: 2-3 business days

Tracking: Available

Next Day Delivery

Delivery Time: 1 business day

Cost: $24.99

Tracking: Available

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide! International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

  • Canada & Mexico: 7-10 business days, $15.99
  • Europe: 10-14 business days, $19.99
  • Asia & Australia: 14-21 business days, $24.99
  • Other regions: Please contact customer service for rates

Note: International orders may be subject to customs fees and import duties, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

During peak seasons: Processing times may be extended by 1-2 additional business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and link to track your package. You can also track your order by:

  • Logging into your account and checking order status
  • Using our Order Tracking page
  • Contacting customer service at support@ecommercestore.com

Shipping Restrictions

Some items may have shipping restrictions:

  • Hazardous Materials: Cannot be shipped internationally or via air
  • Oversized Items: May require special handling and additional fees
  • Perishable Items: Limited to specific shipping methods
  • PO Boxes: Some carriers cannot deliver to PO Box addresses

Delivery Issues

What if my package is delayed?

If your package hasn't arrived within the estimated delivery time, please allow an additional 2-3 business days as delays can occur due to weather, customs, or carrier issues.

What if my package is lost or damaged?

Contact our customer service team immediately at support@ecommercestore.com with your order number and tracking information. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue.

Can I change my shipping address?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact us immediately. Once an order has shipped, we cannot change the delivery address.
